Wand:
	Shapeless 1 x Stick, 1 x Diamond, 2 x XP bottle
	A generic wand, used to craft other wands.

Fireball wand:
	Shapeless: 1 x Wand, 3 x Fire charge
	Casts a ghast fireball when left or right clicked.
	
WaterWalk wand:
	Shapeless: 1 x Wand, 3 x Ice 
	Right click toggles frost walking for any entity.
	
Self healing wand:
	Shapeless: 1 x Wand, 1 x Fermented spider eye, 1 x Wheat, 1 x Milk bucket
	Heals 1.5 HP.
